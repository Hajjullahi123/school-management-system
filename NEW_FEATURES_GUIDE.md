## 1. License Key System
**For the Admin:**
1. Go to **Settings** > **License & Activation**.
2. Enter your license key to activate the system.
3. Once activated, you'll see your package details (Basic, Standard, or Premium).

**For the Developer/Seller:**
- You can generate license keys using the backend API or a script.
- Licenses control the maximum number of students allowed.

## 2. School Branding
**How to Customize:**
1. Go to **Settings** > **School Branding**.
2. **School Info**: Update your school name, motto, address, and contact details.
3. **Visual Identity**:
   - Upload your school logo (PNG recommended).
   - Choose your Primary, Secondary, and Accent colors.
4. Click **Save Changes**.
5. The system will instantly update with your new branding!

## 3. Online Payments (Paystack)
**Setup:**
1. Go to **Settings** > **Payment Integration**.
2. Enable "Online Payments".
3. Enter your Paystack Public and Secret keys (get them from paystack.com).
4. Click **Save Payment Settings**.

**How it Works:**
- **Students**: Log in, go to "My Fees", see their balance, and click "Pay Now".
- **Parents**: Can pay using card, bank transfer, or USSD via Paystack.
- **Accountants**: Payments are automatically recorded and receipts generated.
