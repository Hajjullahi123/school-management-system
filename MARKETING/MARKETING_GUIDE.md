## üéì **SALES TIPS FOR SUCCESS**

1.  **Don't sell features, sell RELIEF.**
    *   *Bad:* "It has a React frontend and Node backend."
    *   *Good:* "It stops you from spending weekends calculating results manually."

2.  **Listen more than you talk.**
    *   Ask: "What is the most annoying part of your day?"
    *   Then show exactly how your software fixes *that specific thing*.

3.  **Use the "Takeaway" Method.**
    *   If they hesitate on price: "I understand. Maybe the Basic package is better for now? You can always upgrade later."

4.  **Speed Wins.**
    *   Reply to emails within 1 hour.
    *   Send proposals within 2 hours of a demo.
